# Money Manager — Simple Expense Tracker for Android

Track daily expenses and income, plan your budget, and see where your money goes — all in one simple app that works offline.

## Features

- **Track Expenses & Income** — Log every transaction with amount, category, date, and note
- **Spending Reports** — Visual pie charts by category with monthly and total balance views
- **Calendar View** — See daily spending on a monthly grid, tap any day for details
- **Google Drive Backup** — Auto-sync your data to Google Drive with up to 10 backup files
- **Works Offline** — No internet required, no account needed, your data stays private
- **Smart Reminders** — Personalized daily reminders with your actual spending data
- **Custom Categories** — Create, edit, and reorder categories with icons
- **14+ Languages** — English, Spanish, Russian, Portuguese, Turkish, Indonesian, French, German, Italian, Vietnamese, Dutch, Catalan, and more
