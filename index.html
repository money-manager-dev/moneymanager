Money Manager

Track your expenses. Know where your money goes.

A personal finance app to manage your money — track daily expenses and income, plan your budget, and see spending reports. Simple, free, and works completely offline.

Why Money Manager?

Managing money is hard. Small purchases add up, and without a clear record, you never know how much you really spend. Money Manager makes it simple — record expenses and income as they happen, manage your money confidently, and make smarter budget decisions.

📊

Daily Expense & Income Tracker

Log every expense and income with amount, category, date, and note. Organize finances with custom categories you create, edit, and reorder.

📈

Budget Reports with Pie Charts

See where your money goes with visual spending reports by category. Switch between monthly and total balance. Drill into any category to see transactions and trends.

📅

Calendar View

See daily expenses and income on a monthly calendar grid. Each day shows totals at a glance — tap any day to view transactions. Customize your week start day.

☁️

Google Drive Backup

Automatically sync your data to Google Drive. Money Manager keeps up to 10 backup files. Also supports device backup — export and restore your complete history with one tap.

🔔

Smart Daily Reminders

Money Manager sends personalized reminders with your actual spending data — not generic notifications. Get a daily summary plus a monthly spending report to review your finances.

📴

Works Completely Offline

No internet required. No account needed. No complex setup. Your financial data stays private and secure on your device. Open the app, log your expense, done.
